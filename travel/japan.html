Japan

Tokyo, Mt. Fuji, Kyoto
March 2023 · ~10 days

Went to Tokyo, Kyoto, and Lake Yamanaka near Mt. Fuji in ~10 days. Was a great time overall, although there were so many tourists during cherry blossom season. I would honestly go during a less busy season next time — it was almost impossible to get nice pictures at the regular locations.

I'd recommend downloading DiDi to order taxis — it was awesome, taxi drivers always came within 2–3 min in Tokyo/Kyoto and you just pay with cash at the end. I looked into Uber but didn't see any cars available in Kyoto.

I'd also recommend getting a metro card (e.g., Suica, Pasmo) and adding it to Apple Wallet — super convenient just flashing your phone instead of digging for a physical card.

Tokyo

Stayed in Shinjuku which I'd personally recommend — it's so lively at night and very easy to take a day trip outside of Tokyo or to other neighborhoods and come back for a convenient dinner before heading home. Tokyo is great for the food, museums, shopping, checking out different neighborhood vibes, and city nightlife.

Mt. Fuji

Staying in a traditional ryokan is awesome — we really liked Rakuyu. The bus is convenient, although we just took taxis. We called local taxi services and I used Google Translate to talk with them in Japanese. Next time I'd try to climb Mt. Fuji (only possible in summer).

Kyoto

The vibe here is a bit quieter than Tokyo, with many more temples and muted colors — really good for reflection and thinking about the history of the place. Stayed in a ryokan here too, but it wasn't as great as Mt. Fuji — a regular hotel is perfectly fine.
