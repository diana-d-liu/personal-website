Taipei

Taiwan
August 2024 · ~2 weeks

Spent 2 weeks here with the primary goal of visiting my younger sister, who was living in Taipei for the whole summer. Most of our time was spent around the green line (MRT), so there are a lot of neighborhoods I didn't get to explore but will do so next time.

Public transit is amazing in Taipei — you can use Google Maps and the instructions will be really clear for which color line to take, which exit to leave the station from, etc. If you don't want to take public transit, Uber is pretty popular in Taipei.

Stayed in Ximen area and really loved it — hotel and Airbnb options are plentiful, inexpensive, modern, and super close to lots of restaurants/gift shops/clothing shops, as well as the Ximen MRT station.

You will need cash for a lot of spots — ATMs are very accessible, you can usually find them in FamilyMarts or 7-11s.
