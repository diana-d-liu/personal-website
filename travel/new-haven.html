New Haven

Connecticut
April 2023 & March 2025

Beautiful and quiet city — visited my sister who's pursuing her PhD at Yale. Both fall and spring are gorgeous on the East Coast.

Kind of hard to get into the city itself. I like flying into NY and taking the Metro-North railroad from Grand Central in Manhattan.

Restaurants & Cafes
Arethusa Farm Dairy Ice cream, milk, cheese — all so good, and nationally ranked!
Atticus Market Cute market with good drinks
BAR Huge pizzas with unique flavors
Da Legna at Nolo Great pizza, a bit more upscale than BAR
Frank Pepe Favorite pizza, so good
G Cafe Bakery Good coffee and baked goods
MOTW Good coffee/chai
Olmo Bagels Great "New Haven-style" bagels
Ordinary Nice bar with unique drinks
Zuppardi's Apizza Outside of New Haven but great, and a famous spot
